About

Sou Yan Ting Antonio is a composer and sound artist based in The Hague. Initially active as a music improviser, Sou became interested in sound as a musical construct emerging through interactions between humans and machines in specific contexts.

He is currently studying at the Institute of Sonology, where his research focuses on language emergence, AI bias, and human-machine co-creation across media.

He is also one-half of Faslane, an electroacoustic project with Matthew Ho formed in 2016. The duo released Wartime Broadcasting Service via Day’s Eye Records and has performed at various experimental music events, including the Lausanne Underground Film and Music Festival (Switzerland), Dao Xuan International Arts Festival (Vietnam), Kill The Silence Festival (Macao), and Twenty Alpha (Hong Kong).
