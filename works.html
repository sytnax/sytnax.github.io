Selected Works

Evolving Speech: Iterative AI Transformations across Languages (2025)

An experiment with natural language processing techniques using a speech feedback loop built with Google Text-to-Speech and Whisper Speech-to-Text.

The system iteratively voices and transcribes text across eight language layers, gradually introducing distortions caused by machine-learning bias. Over multiple cycles the speech evolves, producing emergent phonetic and syntactic transformations.
